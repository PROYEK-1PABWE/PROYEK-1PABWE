Learning Outcomes

Learning Outcomes

  1. Able to show godly attitudes and behaviors to TYME and contribute to improving the quality of community life, nation, state based on Pancasila
  2. Able to cooperate, disciplined, responsible, and character Del: Martuhan, Marroha, Marbisuk
  3. Mastering and understanding theoretical concepts in general from basic mathematics to computers: Discrete mathematics, probability and statistics and calculus can use general theoretical concepts regarding software engineering and software development project management
  4. Able to master and understand Databases, UI/UX design principles and techniques, software security techniques, computer systems for computational soslusion, artificial intelligence and machine learning
  5. Able to apply logical, critical, systematic, and innovative thinking in the or implementation of science and technology that pays attention to and applies humanities values that are in accordance with their area of expertise
  6. Able to perform good and correct software engineering to improve, develop or produce software and compile written reports in Software Engineering
  7. Able to design, apply, and analyze to combine appropriate algorithms to solve problems in the field of computing, consider and decide on security methods or principles
  8. Able to apply, compare, or combine artificial intelligence algorithms, and rules on artificial intelligence to solve problems and software quality assurance processes

Informatics Undergraduate Study Program

About Informatics

The Undergraduate Informatics Study Program (PSIF) at the Del Institute of Technology (IT Del) has a body of knowledge covering the fields of computer science (Computer Science), software engineering (Software Engineering), cyber security (Cybersecurity), and data science (Data Science). Graduates from PSIF are prepared to be able to have problem skills related to the field of computer science and software development ranging from simple to complex and even large analysis.

Vision & Mission

Vision
"To become a superior Informatics Engineering program that plays a role in producing and utilizing technology to develop the local potential for the progress of the nation in 2023."
Mission
  1. Organizing quality, professional, and nationally calculated informatics engineering education.
  2. Conducting research that produces and utilizes technology to develop local potential.
  3. Doing community service in the field of information technology.

Students Association

HIMASTI is a forum for Informatics undergraduate students to be creative, innovate and develop their interests and talents, especially in the field of informatics engineering. HIMASTI has a goal to create Informatics S1 Study Program students who are faithful and pious, independent and honest in attitude, and as a place of creativity who excels and optimizes performance and upholds family values.

Arie Satia Dharma, S.T, M.Kom

Head of S1 Informatics Study Program

Mr. Arie Satia Dharma, S.T, M.Kom is the head of the Bachelor of Informatics study program and chairman of the Senate of the Faculty of Informatics and Electrical Engineering. He is a graduate of Electrical Engineering from Maranatha Christian University and a Masters Degree in Informatics Engineering from USU. He began serving as chairman of the informatics undergraduate study program in 2020.

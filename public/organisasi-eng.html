About HIMASTI DEL Institute of Technology

Himasti is a student organization under the
auspices of the Faculty of Informatics and
Electrical Engineering, Del Institute of Technology.
HIMASTI has a goal to help launch all
educational activities in the department by
participating in maintaining, growing,
improving, all the abilities that exist in Informatics
Students.
In general, the Informatics Student Association
is led by a Chair and a Deputy Chair
who is tasked with coordinating its members
as well as the association itself. The management
of HIMASTI IT Del 2021/2022 is currently led
by Hans Mayson Pranajaya Situmeang as Chair of
HIMASTI IT Del 2021/2022 and Vistar Tiop Rajagukguk
as Deputy Chair of HIMASTI IT Del 2021/2022.

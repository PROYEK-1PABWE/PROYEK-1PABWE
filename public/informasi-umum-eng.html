General Information

INFORMATICS

Informatics is one of the core technologies in the technology system known as Information and Communication Technology, because it is through the functioning of Informatics that software products are obtained that enable the realization of the intelligent nature of Information and Communication Technology. Information and Communication Technology is a strategic enabling technology in supporting efforts to educate the nation's life, in business activities, industry, organizations, the functioning of government, national defense, and in facilitating the convenience of the wider community in the administration of their daily lives.

The Undergraduate Informatics Study Program (PSIF) at the Del Institute of Technology (IT Del) has a body of knowledge covering the areas of computer science (Computer Science), software engineering (Software Engineering), cyber security (Cybersecurity), and data science (Data Science). Graduates from PSIF are prepared to have the ability to analyze problems related to the field of computer science and software development ranging from simple to complex or large-scale. In addition, PSIF graduates are also prepared to have the ability to design and implement the required security in a software. And, lastly, a PSSTI graduate is projected to have the ability to develop intelligent software using a number of artificial intelligence techniques and specifically to have the ability to become an artificial intelligence engineer.
